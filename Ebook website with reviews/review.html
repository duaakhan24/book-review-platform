Book:The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

This book blew me away! The Hunger Games is fast-paced, intense, and emotional. Katniss is such a strong and resilient protagonist. The dystopian setting feels so real, and I couldn’t stop thinking about it even after I finished. It’s definitely one of the best YA books out there, and I highly recommend it to everyone!

- Marnus Stephen
